Data centers designed for AI

Lambda AI factories are modular and interoperable. New GPUs, interconnects, and cooling slots integrate as they ship, so capacity grows without disruptive retrofit.

Optimized rack power and cooling

Lambda AI factories combine direct-to-chip liquid and precision air cooling to sustain peak performance and maximize rack density:

  • Direct-to-chip (DTC) liquid: Improves power usage effectiveness (PUE) by over 15% and enables high-density compute with a roadmap toward 1 MW rack-scale designs.
  • Precision air cooling: Handles CPU and storage loads, as well as any residual heat.
  • Comprehensive redundancy: All critical cooling systems are engineered with redundancy ranging from N+1 to N+N to ensure uninterrupted operation and maximum uptime.

Zero-trust security posture

Lambda’s Superclusters keep your infrastructure secure and under your control:

  • Physical safeguards: Each cluster is secured within a steel cage and protected by multi-stage checkpoints that include biometric verification, RFID access, and two-factor authentication.
  • Logical separation: Maintain full control of cluster access via IPsec VPN, network peering, and fully encrypted storage.
  • Enterprise-grade compliance: SOC 2 Type II certified.
