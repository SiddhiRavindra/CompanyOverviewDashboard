About Mirage

You can't grow your brand without short-form video. At Mirage, we're building foundation models and products to redefine how videos get made. Formerly known as Captions, we rebranded in 2025 to reflect our expanded mission.

A world where the distance between idea and audience is zero, giving creators and businesses the power to reach their full creative and commercial potential.

Our research

We create generative models that turn voice and language into rich, photorealistic video. Our multimodal foundation model produces A-roll footage that looks and feels real, matching speech with natural lip movement, eye contact, and emotional expression. It's a new way to bring human presence to screens, powered entirely by AI.
